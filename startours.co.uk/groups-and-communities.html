Tips for group organisers

  • For first time organisers, use the brochure itinerary as a guide and amend accordingly
  • Have a budget in mind so we can arrange a quotation to suit
  • Make use of our promotional material, knowledge and advice. Please ask if you have any questions
  • If possible, have people in reserve in case of cancellations to avoid charges
  • Consider room arrangements when discussing with your group
  • Consider seat allocation when travelling by coach. A plan can be provided
  • Ask for deposits as early as possible from the members of the group
  • Keep a record of payments made, by whom, when and how much. We can provide you with documents for this
  • Set a deadline for final bookings as this will prompt members to reply
  • Check our confirmation in full or call and double check with one of the team
  • Remember special requests from your group, dietary needs, mobility, etc
  • Remember to ask for final balances at least two weeks before they are due
  • Let us know when your group meets so we can arrange to call you when convenient
