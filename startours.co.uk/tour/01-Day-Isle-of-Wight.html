Tour Itinerary

Tour Itinerary


Day 1
Depart early morning from Wembley and begin your journey to Southampton/Portsmouth. On arrival at Southampton/Portsmouth we take a short ferry ride to the Isle. Later drive to The Needles Landmark where the chairlift (not included) transports visitors from the cliff-top above Alum Bay down to the beach below. Grab a bird’s eye view of multicoloured sand cliffs of Alum Bay and the stunning turquoise waters that stretch as far as the Isle of Wight’s finest and most recognised beaches. Later stop at Sandown Beach which is one the Isle of Wight's finest and most recognised beaches. We then take the ferry back to Southampton/Portsmouth, before arriving back at your drop off point.

Please Note: This is not a guided tour, and only includes transfers

Baggage

Star Tours does not operate a luggage handling service for this tour. Passengers will need to carry their own luggage. Should a luggage hold be present this may be utilised for pushchairs or wheelchairs (if adaptable). Luggage will not be allowed on the vehicle where it exceeds the following: A maximum weight of 10kg for this tour, and not exceeding dimensions of  x 45 x 43cm / 35.5 x 29.5 x 16ins).

In addition, you can also carry one piece of small hand luggage (weighing up to a maximum of 7kg, and not exceeding dimensions of 20 x 20 x 48 cm / 8 x 8 x 19 inches), which can fit under their seat or in the overhead compartment.

All baggage is kept on the vehicle at the owner’s risk.

Children & Young Infants

Car seats are not provided on any of our vehicles, but passengers should carry car seats with travelling infants for comfort and safety.

Pushchairs can be kept in the baggage lockers of the vehicle, although size restrictions may apply.

Wheelchairs

We will accept lightweight manual wheelchairs for travel, subject to them being capable of being stowed in the luggage hold of the vehicle.  It is also essential that a family member is also on tour to push their wheelchair.

In most of Europe, the use of child seats in coaches is mandatory for children under the age of 12 or who are shorter than 150 cm. Children under 7 years old or less than 125 cm tall must use a child seat with a harness or a restraint system. Children between 7 and 12 years old or between 125 and 150 cm tall must use a booster seat or a seat cushion. It is the responsibility of the child's parent or guardian to provide the appropriate child seat for their child.

Coaches

All our vehicles are fitted with seat belts, air-cooling and CD facilities.

All vehicles are either air conditioned or air cooling systems, lowering to a maximum of 8-9ºC from the outside temperature. Only food which is dry and cold can be taken onto the vehicle. Curries, chips and other hot food are not permitted or allowed to be consumed on the vehicle. In addition, chewing gum, burgers, ice cream and other greasy foods are not permitted on the vehicle.

Seat belts must be worn at all times whilst on the vehicle for your own safety.

Distances & Journeys

An important point to note whilst on a touring holiday is that some long journeys are unavoidable especially when travelling from city to city. Star Tours’ recommend passenger’s wear comfortable clothing. It is advisable passengers carry necessities that they may need for the day such as water, dry snacks, cameras etc. with them in their hand luggage.

Other

We highly recommend you go through the general information and Terms & Conditions prior to booking and travelling.

Seating Allocation

Please remember our staff cannot take requests for any seats. Seat numbers will be given to you, on day one of your tour. Passengers with children in their group will not be seated near the front due to EU regulations & company policy. No requests for seats are taken. Medical grounds for a seat are not taken into consideration unless explicitly stated by law.

Timings

Passengers should arrive at their pick up point 15 minutes before the scheduled departure time. Your pick up point, address and time will be stated on your invoice. Passengers arriving late will need to make the necessary arrangements to meet the vehicle at the next suitable location if the connection is missed.

Tour Representatives

Professional drivers/reps accompany our tours throughout the itinerary. Please note that this is not a guided tour, and only includes transfers.

Travel Insurance

Star Tours strongly recommends you purchase travel insurance. Passengers are advised not to carry unnecessary valuables and to be vigilant at all times at tourist spots, as well as on the vehicle. Travel insurance is recommended for all individuals travelling.
