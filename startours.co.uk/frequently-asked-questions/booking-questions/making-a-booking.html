Here's an explanation of the fare options:

  • Children (Aged 11 and Under): Children in this age group traveling with two full fare paying adults are eligible for a discounted rate, making family travel more affordable.

  • Infants (Under 23 Months): Infants in this age category will be charged an additional fee if a separate coach seat is required for them during the journey.

  • Solo Travelers: For individuals traveling alone, a "Single Supplement" fee applies. This fee accounts for the difference in accommodation costs when occupying a room or seat alone, as opposed to sharing with another passenger.

Star Tours follows a dynamic pricing policy, which means that tour prices may vary based on several factors, including the availability of services or the introduction of new offerings. While Star Tours doesn't provide specialized tours exclusively for individuals with mobility challenges, we are committed to making every effort to accommodate them to the best of our ability. Booking with Star Tours is convenient and flexible, and you can choose the method that suits you best:

  • Online Booking: You can make a booking directly on Star Tours' website, which offers a user-friendly online booking system.
  • Booking Form: You may also complete a booking form and send it via email or post to one of Star Tours' offices.
  • Branch Visits: If you prefer a face-to-face interaction, you can visit one of Star Tours' branches. They have branches in various locations, including London, Atlanta, California, Dubai, Ahmedabad, Mumbai, and Chennai.

This flexibility ensures that you can select the booking method that aligns with your preferences and convenience when planning your travel with Star Tours.

You can access the booking form easily on our website by visiting the 'How to Book' section here.

You can conveniently send your completed booking forms to Star Tours by either emailing them to [email protected] or posting them to one of our offices.

Provisional bookings are held in the system for 24 hours. If after 24 hours the booking is not confirmed with a payment then our system will automatically remove it.

A booking reference number is a unique 5-digit identifier assigned to your booking. You can find your booking reference number on the top right-hand side of your booking confirmation. The guarantee of your tour running is contingent upon reaching a minimum number of confirmed travelers. If the minimum number of travelers required for the tour is not met, Star Tours may have to cancel the tour. In the event of tour cancellation, you will be provided with options:

  • Full Refund: Star Tours will refund the full tour cost amount that you have paid.
  • Alternative Travel Date: You can choose to travel on another suitable date if one is available and convenient for you.

It's important to note that Star Tours is not liable to pay for any other services that you may have booked externally to them. Therefore, ensuring that the minimum number of travelers is reached is essential to confirm the tour's operation.

Choosing the right room type is crucial for a comfortable stay during your travels. Here’s a simple guide to help you select the best option for your journey:

  • Single: A room with one bed, subject to a single supplement fee.

  • Twin: Featuring two single beds for your convenience.

  • Double: Equipped with one double bed, which might be composed of two single beds joined together.

  • Triple: Flexible configurations to accommodate three guests, such as one double bed plus one single bed, or three single beds.

  • Double Plus Child: Crafted for the comfort of 2 adults and a child (under 12).

  • Double Plus Child Roll-Out Bed: Suited for 2 adults and a child (under 12), including a roll-out bed.

  • Double Plus Infant: Tailored for 2 adults and an infant (under 2), providing a double bed without an additional bed for the infant.

On European tours, it is generally not possible for four people to stay in the same room, except in the case of infants. If a second child is included and does not require a separate bed, this arrangement may be feasible.
