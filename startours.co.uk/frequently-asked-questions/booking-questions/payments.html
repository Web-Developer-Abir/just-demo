Payments

Back to FAQs

Credit or Debit Card: You can pay with a credit or debit card. Please note that international credit/debit cards issued outside the EU may have a 2% surcharge.

Cheque: You have the option to pay by cheque, but please keep in mind that this method may not be accepted if your tour is within 7 days of travel.

Cash: Cash payments are also accepted.

Bank Transfer: Bank transfers are an option, but they will not be accepted if your tour is within 7 days of travel. For tours within this timeframe, payments must be made by card or in cash.

Business Card: Payments made with a business card will have a 4% surcharge.

The due date for your outstanding balance with Star Tours is contingent on the type of tour booked. For flight tours, the full balance is due 6 weeks before departure; for coach tours, it is due 4 weeks in advance.

Timely payment is vital to prevent booking cancellation, and a £10 admin charge applies for the first reminder and late payments. Should we cancel your tour due to untimely payment, charges will be levied as per our T&Cs.

Please be aware that outstanding balances cannot be deferred due to pending visa processing on any other reason. Ensuring punctual payment is crucial to avoid booking cancellation.

Please make all cheques payable to Star Tours Ltd.

As of January 15th, 2018, there is no surcharge if you are paying with a personal UK or EU issued credit card. However, please be aware that if you choose to pay with a company or corporate card, there will be a surcharge of 4%. For international credit/debit cards, a surcharge of 2% will apply. We encourage you to select the payment method that best suits your needs and circumstances.

A bank transfer, also known as a wire transfer, is a method of electronically transferring funds or payments from one bank account to another bank account. It allows individuals or businesses to move money securely and directly between accounts, typically across different banks or financial institutions. When initiating a bank transfer, it's important to include relevant information, such as your booking reference number, as the payment reference to ensure that the funds are properly allocated to the intended recipient or transaction.

Your money is indeed safe when booking a holiday with Star Tours. Star Tours is a reputable travel company that takes your financial security seriously. They are a member of ABTA (Association of British Travel Agents) and hold an ATOL (Air Tour Operators Licence). This means that your travel arrangements and the funds associated with your booking are 100% financially secure. In the unlikely event of any issues, this accreditation provides you with protection and peace of mind, ensuring that your investment in your holiday is safeguarded. Booking through ABTA and ATOL-accredited organizations like Star Tours is a smart choice to ensure the safety of your travel arrangements and funds.
